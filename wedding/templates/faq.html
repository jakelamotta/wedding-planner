{{gettext('How to get there: ')}}
The easiset way, travelling from outside of Sweden or Finland, is to fly to Arlanda Airport or Skavsta Airport. From Arlanda the best option is to take the bus 801 towards Uppsala (UL, yellow buses). In Uppsala you should transfer to the Eckerö line bus which final destination is the port of Grisslehamn. The bus is included in the ferry ticket. The ferry to the Åland Islands departs three times a day. At 10am, 3pm and 8pm. The bus from Uppsala departs 2h prior to the corresponding ferry departure.
If flying to Skavsta the best option is to take the bus to the Central station of Stockholm and take the corresponding Eckerö line bus from there, that departs 2.5h prior to ferry departure.

How is the weather:
While the average temperature in June is 18° the weather in the Åland Islands can vary so make sure to bring clothes for different types of weather. As we say in Sweden, there are no bad weather, just bad clothing.
What about covid-19:
Finland, as most countries have on and off during the pandemic had different travel restrictions. We don't forsee any problem with entering Finland in June. Hopefully the situation is improved when summer comes but here you can find updated travel restrictions for Åland. Please keep yourself updated and ask us if you have any questions at all. As there will be around 60-80 guests attending the reception and some are in risk groups we encourage all guests to get vaccinated. This will also alleviate issues with travelling.
