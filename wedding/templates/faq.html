{{gettext('How to get there: ')}}
The easiset way, travelling from outside of Sweden or Finland, is to fly to Arlanda Airport or Skavsta Airport. From Arlanda the best option is to take the bus 801 towards Uppsala (UL, yellow buses). In Uppsala you should transfer to the Eckerö line bus which final destination is the port of Grisslehamn. The bus is included in the ferry ticket. The ferry to the Åland Islands departs three times a day. At 10am, 3pm and 8pm. The bus from Uppsala departs 2h prior to the corresponding ferry departure.

If flying to Skavsta the best option is to take the bus to the Central station of Stockholm and take the corresponding Eckerö line bus from there, that departs 2.5h prior to ferry departure.
